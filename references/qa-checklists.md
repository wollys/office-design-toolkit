# QA Checklists

## 1) Content QA
- Objective is explicit.
- Recommendations are actionable.
- KPI/owner/timeline included when relevant.
- No missing sections promised in title/scope.

## 2) Structure QA
- Heading levels are ordered and consistent.
- Sections are balanced (no huge unbroken text blocks).
- Lists/tables used where scan speed benefits.

## 3) Design QA
- Color and typography are consistent.
- Contrast is readable.
- Table headers and row hierarchy are obvious.
- Notes are visually subordinate.

## 4) Technical QA
- File opens without corruption.
- Layout remains stable in common viewers.
- No accidental placeholder text.
- No internal design instructions shown in body.
